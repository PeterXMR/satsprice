Initial release.

- Sats / BTC <-> fiat conversion in a single screen, with an inline
  Sats/BTC selector on the input field.
- Multi-source price aggregation across Coinbase, Kraken, Coingecko,
  and Bitstamp, combined via median with sigma-rejection.
- Navigation drawer: Converter, Price sources, Settings, About.
- Settings: light / dark / follow-system theme, reset selected fiats.
- Background refresh every ~65s; last-known price cached locally for
  offline use.
- All on-device. No backend, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking.
